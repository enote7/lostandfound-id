About Us

Purpose

Welcome to MUBS lost and found ID system, the most comprehesive system devoted exclusively to find and report lost student ids. Our sole purpose is to return lost and found ids to their respective owners through our channel provided by the internet in a timely and efficient manner.

Description

MUBS lost and found ID system offers students a convenient, one stop source for the exchange of lost and found id information on the internet. It features a variety of categories and services that aim at providing students with information about their lost and found ids.

How it works

Like most online databases, students select to enter data using the report buttons. It has excellent verification features which ensures that the lost and found ids are returned to the correct party. Photos and contacts allow even more accurate id retrieval by the rightfully owner. In case someone reports a lost or found id, the admin is able to access an email of the lost and found id therefore ensuring that the right person is able to claim it.
